Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Waldn is a focus app that helps users block selected apps and create focus schedules using Apple Screen Time APIs.

Information We Collect

Waldn stores your schedules, selected blocking settings, daily escape count, and focus statistics locally on your device. This information is used to run the app's core features.

Screen Time Permissions

Waldn uses Apple's FamilyControls, DeviceActivity, and ManagedSettings frameworks so you can select apps to block. Apple provides privacy-preserving tokens for selected apps. Waldn does not receive your full app usage history.

Advertising

Waldn uses Google AdMob to show rewarded ads as part of the unlock flow. Google may collect or process data for advertising, measurement, fraud prevention, and service operation according to Google's policies.

Data Sharing

Waldn does not sell your personal information. Data may be processed by third-party services used by the app, including Google AdMob.

Data Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device. If you delete Waldn, locally stored app data is removed by iOS.

Children

Waldn is intended for personal productivity use and is not designed to collect personal information from children.
